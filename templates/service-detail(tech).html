TECHNOLOGY & CONSULTANCY

KARNATAKA COUNCIL FOR TECHNOLOGY UPGRADATION (KCTU)

Technology upgradation initiatives are implemented through the Karnataka Council for Technology Upgradation.

Karnataka Council for Technological upgradation has commenced functioning from 1996. KCTU is a joint venture of Govt. of India, Govt. of Karnataka and Industries Association.

It functions as a Nodal Agency for Technology Transfer to MSEs. The Council functions under the Chairmanship of the Commissioner for Industrial Development and Director of Industries and Commerce, Govt. of Karnataka.

The main objectives of the KCTU

To enhance competitive status of SMEâ€™s catalyze technology upgradation through acquisition, adoption and modernization to reduce cost of productivity, quality improvement to make the SMEs products more competitive both nationally and internationally.

It assists the SMEs to obtain ISO 9001 / 14000 or any other nationally and internationally recognized Certification.

The Council is a Nodal Agency for creating awareness and provides assistance for registration of Intellectual Property Rights, guidance in Plant Layout, diversification, modernization and expansion.

TECHNICAL CONSULTANCY SERVICES ORGANISATION OF KARNATAKA [TECSOK]

Karnataka has a number of international, national and domestic consultants both at the individual level and as organizations. The Govt. has established TECSOK to provide expert consultancy to investors.

TECSOK has a team of experienced consultants who can help entrepreneurs in project formulation and implementation.

VISVESVARAYA TRADE PROMOTION CENTRE (VTPC)

VTPC was established in the year 1965. It is a nodal agency for promotion of exports in the State. It is conducting training programmes in management of export units & export related programmes. VTPC is actively involved in promotion of export in the State.

Capacity Building programmes:

In association with the local DIC / Chamber of Commerce / Industry Association / Exim Bank, VTPC is conducting Export Awareness Programmes, Export Management Training Programmes, Seminars / Workshops and Interaction meetings at the District / Taluk level.

Market Development Assistance:

To promote Exports in the International level, exporters /traders delegations from the State were extended MDA assistance to participate in the International Exhibitions/B2B /Market Research.

WTO / IPR programmes:

The objective of WTO & IPR is to create awareness on local and International trade laws, policies & guidelines, Create awareness of Intellectual Property Rights, etc.
