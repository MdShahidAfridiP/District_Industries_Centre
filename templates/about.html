About Us

⭐ The District Industries Centre Ballari, headed by Joint Director is responsible for the industrial development and growth of Industries in the district.

⭐ The role of DIC Ballari is to promote entrepreneurship and provide handholding support to all the industries in the district, particularly the MSMEs.

⭐ The major function of DIC is the implementation of the State Industrial policy and implementation of various State and Central government schemes at the district level.

⭐ The Joint Director of DIC is supported by a team of Deputy Director and Assistant Directors and other staff.

⭐ The DIC is also responsible for providing infrastructure facilities to industries with the help of various boards and corporations like KIADB and KSSIDC.

⭐ To promote entrepreneurship in Ballari.

⭐ To create a favourable climate for industrial growth.

⭐ To encourage adoption of environment friendly and sustainable technologies.
