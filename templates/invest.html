  • SECTORIAL ADVANTAGES

⭐ Ballari, also known as Steel City of Karnataka is home to a number of iron and steel industries and sponge iron industries. The illustrious list includes the likes of JSW Steel Ltd, SMIORE, BMM Ispat, Minera Steels, A1 Steels among others. A number of MSMEs are also present in this sphere which support these large industries. In addition to being recognised as the Steel City, Ballari is well known for its Jeans and other Readymade Garments. Thousands of MSMEs are working on manufacturing jeans and they manufacture and supply garments to all the leading brands in the country.

 

⭐ The availability of natural resources and skilled manpower make it an attractive investment destination. The city is well connected by Road, Rail and Air. The district is home to numerous engineering and ITI college which produce technical graduates for industries. Also traditional artisans and women form a large part of the work force in the industries. The Industries and Commerce department has acquired and developed ready to occupy land bank. The district has also been identified as a Textile Cluster under the “Compete with China” programme of the Karnataka State government and a vision group has been constituted to develop a holistic textile cluster.

 

⭐ All these factors make Ballari a sought after destination to invest into industrial activity.
